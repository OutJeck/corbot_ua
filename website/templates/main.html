About Covid-19 map

This is a map based on OpenStreetMap. It is a map of the world, which is painted in different shades of white, yellow and red. Each color demonstrates the number of patients with covid per one million in the country. This is done to improve the analysis, as some countries have small populations but a high percentage of victims. Therefore, the calculation of the victims per one million will perfectly demonstrate the percentage dependence of patients around the world.

To talk less, it is better to look with your own eyes.

About Corbot

Corbot is a bot based in the telegram application. It will help you learn the latest information about the coronavirus of selected countries. You will be able to build a graph comparing the two countries in terms of the number of patients. And, most importantly, subscribe to the daily newsletter, where you can add and remove from the list of countries you follow. Every day you will receive a message with general statistics on the number of deaths, illnesses and people cured. Just scroll by pressing the Corbot button.

About EpidemLab

This application simulates a disease spread with you parameters. It is simple yet versatile and effectively shows and visualizes the dependency of the spread of a disease on its parameters and mitigation measures. In summary, there are 13 parameters, 10 of which you can enter by yourself.
